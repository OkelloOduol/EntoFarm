EntoFarm Gallery

Explore our journey from waste to value — showcasing Black Soldier Fly (BSF) rearing, sustainable feed production, and our team in action.

Waste Collection & Sorting

Organic waste is collected and sorted to provide clean feedstock for BSF larvae, turning waste into a valuable resource.

Larval Rearing Units

Our controlled rearing units nurture millions of BSF larvae that transform organic matter into high-value protein.

Harvesting Process

Mature larvae are harvested and processed for use in poultry, pig, and fish feed — a sustainable protein alternative.

Packaging & Supply

We hygienically package BSF products for farmers and feed manufacturers across Kenya — ensuring freshness and quality.

EctoFarm Team

Meet the people driving innovation in circular agriculture — passionate about sustainability and community impact.

On-Farm Applications

BSF larvae are used directly on our farm to feed poultry and pigs, closing the nutrient loop within EctoFarm’s ecosystem.
