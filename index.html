Our Products & Services

BSF Larvae (Fresh / Dried)

Our high-quality Black Soldier Fly larvae are rich in protein (40–45%), healthy fats, and essential minerals such as calcium and phosphorus. They serve as a natural, affordable, and sustainable feed for poultry, pigs, and fish. Available in both fresh and dried forms, our larvae promote faster animal growth and improved health while reducing farmers’ reliance on costly imported feeds.

BSF Frass (Organic Fertilizer)

BSF frass is a natural byproduct of our larvae rearing process and acts as an excellent organic fertilizer for crops. It enriches soil with nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, improving soil texture and boosting plant growth. Farmers use EctoFarm frass to replace synthetic fertilizers, ensuring healthier, chemical-free produce and long-term soil fertility.

EctoFarm demonstrates the power of circular agriculture by combining insect farming with animal production and crop cultivation. Benefits

01 For Farmers: Affordable, Sustainable Protein Source

BSF larvae provide a cost-effective and nutrient-dense alternative to traditional animal feeds like fishmeal or soy. Farmers using BSF-based feeds experience improved livestock productivity at a fraction of the cost. The larvae are rich in protein, fat, and essential amino acids — all critical for healthy poultry, pigs, and fish. By adopting this sustainable feed option, farmers can reduce dependence on expensive imports and stabilize their production costs.

02 For the Environment: Waste Reduction & Emission Control

BSF farming helps convert organic waste into valuable resources instead of letting it rot in landfills. This reduces methane and carbon emissions while preventing harmful leachate from contaminating soil and water. Every kilogram of waste processed by BSF larvae represents a meaningful step toward a cleaner, greener planet. EctoFarm actively contributes to Kenya’s climate resilience and sustainable waste management goals.

03 For Animals: Better Nutrition & Feed Efficiency

Animals fed with BSF-based protein show better feed conversion ratios, faster growth rates, and stronger immunity. The larvae’s balanced composition of protein, lipids, and minerals supports healthy digestion and growth. Whether used for poultry, pigs, or fish, BSF feed improves animal health naturally — reducing the need for antibiotics and synthetic supplements.

04 For the Economy: Job Creation & Circular Income Loops

BSF farming is a catalyst for green jobs and local enterprise development. From waste collectors to feed processors, the entire value chain creates steady employment opportunities for youth and women. By turning waste into wealth, EctoFarm supports circular economies that boost rural incomes, strengthen communities, and contribute to national food security.

How It Works

01 Waste Collection & Sorting

EctoFarm begins the process by collecting biodegradable waste such as kitchen scraps, crop residues, and agro-industrial byproducts from local sources. This waste is carefully sorted to remove non-organic materials like plastics or metals, ensuring a clean and safe environment for the larvae to feed and grow. By recycling organic waste, we help reduce landfill buildup and greenhouse gas emissions while creating a valuable raw material for sustainable feed production.

02 BSF Breeding & Larval Rearing

Once the organic waste is prepared, it is introduced into our BSF breeding units where adult flies lay eggs. These eggs hatch into larvae, which feed vigorously on the organic material, converting waste into nutrient-rich biomass within days. EctoFarm maintains ideal humidity and temperature conditions to ensure optimal larval growth, resulting in strong, protein-dense larvae ready for harvest.

03 Harvesting & Drying

When the larvae reach maturity, they are separated from the compost and collected for processing. The harvested larvae are either used fresh or dried depending on the intended use. Drying enhances storage life, reduces moisture, and concentrates nutrients, making it easier to transport and incorporate into animal feed formulations for poultry, pigs, and fish.

04 Processing, Packaging & Supply

The processed BSF larvae and frass are hygienically packaged in eco-friendly containers, ensuring freshness and quality from farm to customer. Our packaging includes clear labeling for easy use and product traceability. EctoFarm supplies both individual farmers and bulk feed producers, ensuring timely delivery of high-quality insect protein and organic fertilizer that supports sustainable agriculture across Kenya.
